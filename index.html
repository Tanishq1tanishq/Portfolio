How it lands

Four steps,
scroll to follow.

01
Brief — what does success look like?
01 · Brief

Translate the ask into outcomes.

Before a single deliverable, I write the success criteria. KPIs, scope, non-goals. The brief becomes the contract.

02 · Plan

Milestones with dates that bind.

A clear plan with risk register, dependencies, and review gates. Estimates are honest, not optimistic.

03 · Build

Execute, document, recalibrate.

Weekly check-ins with the team. Weekly recalibration with reality. Documentation always shipped alongside the artefact.

04 · Land

Measure, then close it out.

Outcomes verified against the brief. Lessons documented. The next project starts with shoulders standing on this one.
